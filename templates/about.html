FAQs

What is steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within non-secret data, such as images, making it undetectable to the naked eye.

How secure is the tool?

We use advanced encryption and steganographic techniques to ensure your data is highly secure and tamper-proof.

How do I get started?
